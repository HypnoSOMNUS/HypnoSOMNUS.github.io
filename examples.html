例题 7-1：矩形回路的感应电动势

原题

A uniform magnetic field \( \vec B \) vertical through a rectangular ring with a length of \(a\) and a width of \(b\), as shown in figure. In the following three cases, solve the induction electromotive force.

  1. \(\vec B=\vec e_zB_0\cos(\omega t)\)，rectangular loop is static.
  2. \(\vec B=\vec e_zB_0\)，the width \(b\) equals a constant, but the long side increases with time because sliding conductor \(L\) moves with velocity \(\vec v=\vec e_xv\).
  3. \(\vec B=\vec e_zB_0\cos(\omega t)\)，and sliding conductor \(L\) moves with velocity \(\vec v=\vec e_xv\).

详细作答

第 1 种情况：回路静止，磁通变化只来自 \(B(t)\)。

\[ \Phi=abB_0\cos(\omega t) \]

\[ \varepsilon_{in}=-\frac{d\Phi}{dt} =-\frac{d}{dt}[abB_0\cos(\omega t)] =\omega abB_0\sin(\omega t) \]

第 2 种情况：磁场不变，电动势来自导体运动。

\[ \varepsilon_{in}=\oint_C(\vec v\times\vec B)\cdot d\vec l =\int_C(\vec e_xv\times\vec e_zB_0)\cdot\vec e_y\,dl =-vB_0b \]

也可用磁通法。面积随时间为 \(S=bvt\)，因此

\[ \varepsilon_{in}=-\frac{d}{dt}(bB_0vt)=-bB_0v \]

第 3 种情况：磁场变化与导体运动同时存在，两项相加：

\[ \varepsilon_{in} =-\int_S\frac{\partial\vec B}{\partial t}\cdot d\vec S +\oint_C(\vec v\times\vec B)\cdot d\vec l \]

此时面积 \(S=bvt\)，代入得

\[ \varepsilon_{in} =vt\,\omega bB_0\sin(\omega t)-vbB_0\cos(\omega t) \]

第一项是变压器电动势，第二项是运动电动势。

例题 7-2：由瞬时场写复矢量

原题

Find the complex vector form of the following time-harmonic field:

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\vec e_xE_{xm}\cos(\omega t-kz+\phi_x) +\vec e_yE_{ym}\sin(\omega t-kz+\phi_y) \]

详细作答

复矢量约定为

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\operatorname{Re}[\vec E_m(z)e^{j\omega t}] \]

先把正弦改写成余弦：

\[ \sin(\omega t-kz+\phi_y)= \cos(\omega t-kz+\phi_y-\pi/2) \]

因此

\[ \vec E(z,t)= \operatorname{Re}\left[ \vec e_xE_{xm}e^{j(\omega t-kz+\phi_x)} +\vec e_yE_{ym}e^{j(\omega t-kz+\phi_y-\pi/2)} \right] \]

取出公共因子 \(e^{j\omega t}\)，得复矢量：

\[ \vec E_m(z) =\vec e_xE_{xm}e^{j(-kz+\phi_x)} +\vec e_yE_{ym}e^{j(-kz+\phi_y-\pi/2)} \]

也可写成

\[ \vec E_m(z)= (\vec e_xE_{xm}e^{j\phi_x} -j\vec e_yE_{ym}e^{j\phi_y})e^{-jkz} \]

例题 7-3：由复矢量求瞬时场

原题

The complex vector of electric field intensity is

\[ \vec E_m(z)=\vec e_xjE_{xm}\cos(k_zz) \]

\(k_z\) and \(E_{xm}\) are constant values. Find the instantaneous value of the electric field intensity.

详细作答

按复矢量定义：

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\operatorname{Re}[\vec E_m(z)e^{j\omega t}] =\operatorname{Re}[\vec e_xjE_{xm}\cos(k_zz)e^{j\omega t}] \]

因为 \(j=e^{j\pi/2}\)，所以

\[ \vec E(z,t)= \vec e_xE_{xm}\cos(k_zz)\cos(\omega t+\pi/2) \]

最终

\[ \boxed{\vec E(z,t)=-\vec e_xE_{xm}\cos(k_zz)\sin(\omega t)} \]

例题 8-1：由磁场复矢量求平面波参数

原题

The complex expression magnetic field intensity of plane wave in air is

\[ \vec H=(-\vec e_xA+\vec e_y2+\vec e_z4)e^{-j\pi(4x+3z)} \]

Where \(A\) is constant. Get: 1) \(\vec k\); 2) wavelength and frequency; 3) the value of \(A\); 4) the complex expression electric field.

详细作答

与标准形式 \( \vec H=\vec H_me^{-j\vec k\cdot\vec r} \) 比较：

\[ \vec k\cdot\vec r=4\pi x+3\pi z \Rightarrow \vec k=4\pi\vec e_x+3\pi\vec e_z \]

\[ k=|\vec k|=\sqrt{(4\pi)^2+(3\pi)^2}=5\pi \]

所以

\[ \lambda=\frac{2\pi}{k}=\frac{2}{5}\text{ m}, \qquad f=\frac{c}{\lambda}=7.5\times10^8\text{ Hz} \]

平面波为横波，故 \( \vec k\cdot\vec H_m=0 \)：

\[ 4\pi(-A)+3\pi\cdot4=0 \Rightarrow A=3 \]

传播方向单位矢量为

\[ \vec e_n=\frac{\vec k}{k}=\frac45\vec e_x+\frac35\vec e_z \]

空气中 \( \eta_0=120\pi\Omega \)，且 \( \vec E=\eta_0\vec H\times\vec e_n \)。代入 \(\vec H_m=-3\vec e_x+2\vec e_y+4\vec e_z\)：

\[ \vec H_m\times\vec e_n =1.2\vec e_x+5\vec e_y-1.6\vec e_z \]

\[ \boxed{\vec E =120\pi(1.2\vec e_x+5\vec e_y-1.6\vec e_z)e^{-j\pi(4x+3z)}\text{ V/m}} \]

例题 8-2：判断均匀平面波极化方式

原题

Illustrate the following uniform plane wave polarization mode.

  1. \(\vec E=\vec e_xE_me^{-jkz}-\vec e_yjE_me^{-jkz}\)
  2. \(\vec E=\vec e_xE_m\sin(\omega t-kz+\pi/4)+\vec e_yE_m\cos(\omega t-kz-\pi/4)\)
  3. \(\vec E=\vec e_xE_m\sin(\omega t+kz)+\vec e_y2E_m\cos(\omega t+kz)\)

详细作答

判断极化看两个正交分量的幅值和初相位差 \( \Delta\phi=\phi_y-\phi_x \)。

第 1 小题：传播沿 \(+z\)，\(E_{xm}=E_{ym}\)，\(\phi_x=0,\phi_y=-\pi/2\)。

\[ \Delta\phi=-\frac{\pi}{2} \]

幅值相等、相位差为 \(-\pi/2\)，按课件约定为右旋圆极化：RHCP。

第 2 小题：令 \(\psi=\omega t-kz\)，则

\[ \sin(\psi+\pi/4)=\cos(\psi-\pi/4) \]

两个分量都等价为相位 \(-\pi/4\)，所以 \(\Delta\phi=0\)，为线极化。

第 3 小题：相位为 \(\omega t+kz\)，传播沿 \(-z\)。

\[ E_x=E_m\cos(\omega t+kz-\pi/2),\qquad E_y=2E_m\cos(\omega t+kz) \]

幅值不相等，\(\Delta\phi=\pi/2\)，按课件结论为右旋椭圆极化：RH elliptical polarized wave。

例题 8-3：海水中的有耗平面波

原题

Polarized in the \(x\) direction waves propagating in sea along \(+z\). The parameters of the sea are \(\varepsilon_r=81,\mu_r=1,\sigma=4\text{ S/m}\). At \(z=0\), \(E_x=100\cos(10^7\pi t)\text{ V/m}\). Please work out:

  1. attenuation constant, phase constant, intrinsic impedance, phase velocity, wavelength and skin depth;
  2. the distance when the amplitude is reduced to \(1/1000\) of the value at \(z=0\);
  3. instantaneous electric and magnetic field at \(z=0.8\text{ m}\);
  4. average power per \(1\text{ m}^2\) at \(z=0.8\text{ m}\).

详细作答

角频率 \(\omega=10^7\pi\)，频率

\[ f=\frac{\omega}{2\pi}=5\times10^6\text{ Hz} \]

先判断介质：

\[ \frac{\sigma}{\omega\varepsilon} \approx180\gg1 \]

因此海水可视为良导体。良导体近似：

\[ \alpha=\sqrt{\pi f\mu\sigma} =\sqrt{\pi\cdot5\times10^6\cdot4\pi\times10^{-7}\cdot4} =8.89\text{ Np/m} \]

\[ \beta=\alpha=8.89\text{ rad/m} \]

复波阻抗为

\[ \eta_c=\sqrt{\frac{\omega\mu}{\sigma}}e^{j\pi/4} =\pi e^{j\pi/4}\Omega \]

相速度、波长、皮肤深度为

\[ v_p=\frac{\omega}{\beta} =\frac{10^7\pi}{8.89}=3.53\times10^6\text{ m/s} \]

\[ \lambda=\frac{2\pi}{\beta}=0.707\text{ m}, \qquad \delta=\frac{1}{\alpha}=0.112\text{ m} \]

幅度衰减到 \(1/1000\)：

\[ e^{-\alpha z}=\frac{1}{1000} \Rightarrow z=\frac{\ln1000}{8.89}=0.777\text{ m} \]

电场瞬时表达式为

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\vec e_x100e^{-8.89z}\cos(10^7\pi t-8.89z)\text{ V/m} \]

在 \(z=0.8\text{ m}\) 处：

\[ \boxed{\vec E(0.8,t)=\vec e_x0.082\cos(10^7\pi t-7.11)\text{ V/m}} \]

磁场滞后电场 \(45^\circ\)，幅值除以 \(|\eta_c|=\pi\)：

\[ \boxed{\vec H(0.8,t)=\vec e_y0.026\cos(10^7\pi t-1.61)\text{ A/m}} \]

平均坡印廷矢量：

\[ \vec S_{av} =\vec e_z\frac{1}{2|\eta_c|}E_{xm}^2e^{-2\alpha z}\cos\frac{\pi}{4} \]

代入 \(z=0.8\text{ m}\)：

\[ \boxed{\vec S_{av}=\vec e_z0.75\text{ mW/m}^2} \]

穿过 \(1\text{ m}^2\) 的平均功率为 \(0.75\text{ mW}\)。

例题 8-4：聚乙烯中无耗平面波

原题

9.4 GHz uniform plane wave propagation in polyethylene, assuming lossless medium, \(\varepsilon_r=2.26\). The amplitude of magnetic field is \(7\text{ mA/m}\). Get phase velocity, wavelength, wave impedance and amplitude of electric field.

详细作答

无耗非磁介质中

\[ v=\frac{c}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_r}} =\frac{3\times10^8}{\sqrt{2.26}} =1.996\times10^8\text{ m/s} \]

\[ \eta=\frac{\eta_0}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_r}} =\frac{377}{\sqrt{2.26}} =251\Omega \]

电场幅值：

\[ E_m=H_m\eta=7\times10^{-3}\times251=1.757\text{ V/m} \]

按题干 \(f=9.4\text{ GHz}\)：

\[ \lambda=\frac{v}{f} =\frac{1.996\times10^8}{9.4\times10^9} =2.12\times10^{-2}\text{ m} \]

课件页中若显示 \(2.12\text{ m}\)，与 GHz 频率不一致，应为 \(2.12\text{ cm}\)。

例题 8-5：由磁场、传播方向写出 \(E,H\)

原题

The amplitude of magnetic field of uniform plane wave is \(1/(3\pi)\text{ A/m}\), phase constant is \(30\text{ rad/m}\), spread in air along the \(-\vec e_z\) direction. If the direction of \(\vec H\) is \(-\vec e_y\), write out \( \vec E,\vec H \), and the frequency and wavelength.

详细作答

沿 \(-z\) 传播，相位写为 \(\omega t+\beta z\)。

\[ \vec H(z,t)=-\vec e_y\frac{1}{3\pi}\cos(\omega t+30z)\text{ A/m} \]

空气中 \(\eta_0=120\pi\Omega\)，因此

\[ E_m=\eta_0H_m=120\pi\cdot\frac{1}{3\pi}=40\text{ V/m} \]

为使 \(\vec E\times\vec H\) 指向 \(-z\)，\(\vec E\) 沿 \(+x\)：

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\vec e_x40\cos(\omega t+30z)\text{ V/m} \]

由 \(\beta=30\)：

\[ \lambda=\frac{2\pi}{30}=0.21\text{ m} \]

\[ f=\frac{c}{\lambda} =\frac{3\times10^8}{\pi/15} =1.43\times10^9\text{ Hz} \]

例题 8-6：由给定最大值确定瞬时场

原题

100 MHz uniform EM wave propagation in lossless medium \((\varepsilon_r=4,\mu_r=1)\) along \(+z\). Electric field is in \(+\vec e_x\) direction. When \(t=0\) and \(z=1/8\text{ m}\), the amplitude of electric field is \(10^{-4}\text{ V/m}\). Find the instantaneous value of electric and magnetic field.

详细作答

设

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\vec e_x10^{-4}\cos(\omega t-\beta z+\phi) \]

频率 \(f=100\text{ MHz}=10^8\text{ Hz}\)，所以

\[ \omega=2\pi f=2\pi\times10^8\text{ rad/s} \]

相位常数

\[ \beta=\frac{\omega}{c}\sqrt{\varepsilon_r\mu_r} =\frac{2\pi\times10^8}{3\times10^8}\cdot2 =\frac{4\pi}{3}\text{ rad/m} \]

在 \(t=0,z=1/8\text{ m}\) 时电场达到最大，故

\[ -\beta\frac18+\phi=0 \Rightarrow \phi=\frac{\beta}{8}=\frac{\pi}{6} \]

因此

\[ \boxed{\vec E(z,t)=\vec e_x10^{-4}\cos\left(2\pi\times10^8t-\frac{4\pi}{3}z+\frac{\pi}{6}\right)\text{ V/m}} \]

也可写为

\[ \vec E(z,t)=\vec e_x10^{-4}\cos\left[2\pi\times10^8t-\frac{4\pi}{3}\left(z-\frac18\right)\right]\text{ V/m} \]

介质波阻抗

\[ \eta=\frac{\eta_0}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_r}}=60\pi\Omega \]

沿 \(+z\) 传播且 \(E\parallel x\)，故 \(H\parallel y\)：

\[ \boxed{\vec H(z,t)=\vec e_y\frac{10^{-4}}{60\pi} \cos\left[2\pi\times10^8t-\frac{4\pi}{3}\left(z-\frac18\right)\right]\text{ A/m}} \]

例题 8-7：能流、能量密度与损耗功率密度计算模板

原题

Ex: The calculation of the instantaneous Poynting vector. Ex: Calculation of the electric field energy density of instantaneous storage. Ex: Calculation of instantaneous power density consumption. When solving the problems, the instantaneous expression of the field must be written.

详细作答

若题目要求瞬时量，必须先写出 \(\vec E(\vec r,t)\)、\(\vec H(\vec r,t)\)。

瞬时坡印廷矢量：

\[ \vec S(\vec r,t)=\vec E(\vec r,t)\times\vec H(\vec r,t) \]

瞬时电场能量密度：

\[ w_E(\vec r,t)=\frac12\vec D(\vec r,t)\cdot\vec E(\vec r,t) =\frac12\varepsilon|\vec E(\vec r,t)|^2 \]

瞬时焦耳损耗功率密度：

\[ p_J(\vec r,t)=\vec J(\vec r,t)\cdot\vec E(\vec r,t) =\sigma|\vec E(\vec r,t)|^2 \]

若题目要求时谐平均量，可直接用复矢量：

\[ \vec S_{av}=\frac12\operatorname{Re}(\vec E\times\vec H^*) \]

\[ w_{E,av}=\frac14\operatorname{Re}(\vec D\cdot\vec E^*) =\frac14\varepsilon|\vec E|^2 \]

\[ w_{M,av}=\frac14\operatorname{Re}(\vec B\cdot\vec H^*) =\frac14\mu|\vec H|^2 \]

\[ p_{J,av}=\frac12\operatorname{Re}(\sigma\vec E\cdot\vec E^*) =\frac12\sigma|\vec E|^2 \]

例题 8-8：自由空间平面波穿过圆面的平均功率

原题

The electric field intensity of plane wave in free space is

\[ \vec E=\vec e_x50\cos(\omega t-kz)\text{ V/m} \]

Find the average power vertical cross a round plane with radius \(2.5\text{ m}\) at \(z=z_0\).

详细作答

复矢量为

\[ \vec E=\vec e_x50e^{-jkz} \]

自由空间波阻抗 \(\eta_0=120\pi\Omega\)，所以

\[ \vec H=\vec e_y\frac{50}{120\pi}e^{-jkz} =\vec e_y\frac{5}{12\pi}e^{-jkz}\text{ A/m} \]

平均坡印廷矢量：

\[ \vec S_{av}= \frac12\operatorname{Re}(\vec E\times\vec H^*) =\vec e_z\frac12\cdot50\cdot\frac{5}{12\pi} =\vec e_z\frac{125}{12\pi}\text{ W/m}^2 \]

圆面面积 \(A=\pi R^2=\pi(2.5)^2\)，故

\[ \boxed{P_{av}=S_{av}A =\frac{125}{12\pi}\pi(2.5)^2 =65.1\text{ W}} \]

例题 8-9：铜板屏蔽室厚度

原题

In electromagnetic measurement, in order to prevent indoor electronic equipment from outside electromagnetic field, copper plate can be used to construct shielding room. Usually require copper thickness greater than \(5\delta\). If the required frequency range is from \(10\text{ kHz}\) to \(100\text{ MHz}\), calculate how thick copper plate at least. Cu parameters: \(\mu=\mu_0,\varepsilon=\varepsilon_0,\sigma=5.8\times10^7\text{ S/m}\).

详细作答

铜在该频段可视为良导体，皮肤深度为

\[ \delta=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\pi f\mu\sigma}} \]

低端频率 \(f_L=10\text{ kHz}=10^4\text{ Hz}\)：

\[ \delta_L= \frac{1}{\sqrt{\pi\cdot10^4\cdot4\pi\times10^{-7}\cdot5.8\times10^7}} =0.66\text{ mm} \]

高端频率 \(f_H=100\text{ MHz}=10^8\text{ Hz}\)：

\[ \delta_H= \frac{1}{\sqrt{\pi\cdot10^8\cdot4\pi\times10^{-7}\cdot5.8\times10^7}} =6.6\mu\text{m} \]

频率越低皮肤深度越大，因此按 \(f_L\) 设计：

\[ \boxed{d\ge5\delta_L=3.3\text{ mm}} \]

例题 8-10：正入射到理想导体平板

原题

Uniform plane wave propagates along \(+z\). The electric field intensity vector is

\[ \vec E_i=\vec e_x100\sin(\omega t-\beta z)+\vec e_y200\cos(\omega t-\beta z)\text{ V/m} \]

Find:

  1. the corresponding magnetic field intensity;
  2. if at \(z=0\) an infinite ideal conducting plate is placed, write electric and magnetic fields in \(z<0\);
  3. the electric current density on the surface of ideal conductor;
  4. state the polarization mode of incident wave and reflected wave.

详细作答

先将入射电场写成复矢量。因为 \(\sin\psi=\cos(\psi-\pi/2)\)：

\[ \vec E_i(z) =\vec e_x100e^{-j\beta z}e^{-j\pi/2} +\vec e_y200e^{-j\beta z} \]

沿 \(+z\) 传播，所以

\[ \vec H_i(z)=\frac{1}{\eta_0}\vec e_z\times\vec E_i =\frac{1}{\eta_0}\left[ -\vec e_x200e^{-j\beta z} +\vec e_y100e^{-j\beta z}e^{-j\pi/2} \right] \]

瞬时形式：

\[ \vec H_i(z,t)=\frac{1}{\eta_0} \left[-\vec e_x200\cos(\omega t-\beta z) +\vec e_y100\cos(\omega t-\beta z-\pi/2)\right] \]

理想导体表面切向电场为零，所以在 \(z=0\) 处 \(\vec E_r=-\vec E_i\)。反射波沿 \(-z\) 传播：

\[ \vec E_r(z)= -\vec e_x100e^{j\beta z}e^{-j\pi/2} -\vec e_y200e^{j\beta z} \]

\[ \vec H_r(z)=\frac{1}{\eta_0}(-\vec e_z\times\vec E_r) =\frac{1}{\eta_0}\left[ -\vec e_x200e^{j\beta z} +\vec e_y100e^{j\beta z}e^{-j\pi/2} \right] \]

总场为入射波与反射波叠加：

\[ \vec E_1(z)=\vec E_i+\vec E_r =-\vec e_xj200e^{-j\pi/2}\sin(\beta z)-\vec e_yj400\sin(\beta z) \]

\[ \vec H_1(z)=\vec H_i+\vec H_r =\frac{1}{\eta_0}\left[ -\vec e_x400\cos(\beta z) +\vec e_y200e^{-j\pi/2}\cos(\beta z) \right] \]

导体表面电流密度：

\[ \vec J_s=-\vec e_z\times\vec H_1\big|_{z=0} =\vec e_x\frac{200}{\eta_0}e^{-j\pi/2} +\vec e_y\frac{400}{\eta_0} \]

\[ \boxed{\vec J_s=-\vec e_xj0.53+\vec e_y1.06\text{ A/m}} \]

极化：入射波两个正交分量幅值不等，相位差为 \(\pi/2\)，沿 \(+z\) 传播，故为左旋椭圆极化；反射波沿 \(-z\) 传播，按课件约定为右旋椭圆极化。

例题 8-11：由驻波比和波长比求介质参数

原题

In free space, uniform plane wave vertical incidence to the semi-infinite plane of lossless dielectric. Given SWR \(=3\) in free space, the wavelength in the dielectric is \(1/6\) of that in free space. The point of minimum value of standing wave is on the interface. Work out the relative permeability and relative permittivity of the dielectric.

详细作答

由驻波比

\[ S=\frac{1+|\Gamma|}{1-|\Gamma|}=3 \Rightarrow |\Gamma|=\frac12 \]

电场最小点在界面，说明界面处反射电场与入射电场反相：

\[ \Gamma=-\frac12 \]

反射系数

\[ \Gamma=\frac{\eta_2-\eta_1}{\eta_2+\eta_1} \]

区域 1 是自由空间，\(\eta_1=\eta_0\)，代入 \(\Gamma=-1/2\)：

\[ \eta_2=\frac13\eta_0 \]

又

\[ \eta_2=\eta_0\sqrt{\frac{\mu_r}{\varepsilon_r}} \Rightarrow \frac{\mu_r}{\varepsilon_r}=\frac19 \]

波长关系：

\[ \lambda_2=\frac{\lambda_0}{\sqrt{\mu_r\varepsilon_r}}=\frac{\lambda_0}{6} \Rightarrow \mu_r\varepsilon_r=36 \]

联立两式：

\[ \boxed{\mu_r=2,\qquad\varepsilon_r=18} \]

例题 8-12：正入射到无耗介质，求透射场

原题

The electric field of incidence wave is \( \vec E_i=\vec e_x10\cos(3\pi\times10^9t-10\pi z)\text{ V/m} \). It vertically incidents to the interface at \(z=0\) from region \(z<0\) of free space to region \(z>0\) with \(\mu_r=1,\varepsilon_r=4\). Find the electric and magnetic fields in \(z>0\).

详细作答

区域 1 为自由空间：

\[ \eta_1=\eta_0=120\pi\Omega \]

区域 2 的波阻抗：

\[ \eta_2=\eta_0\sqrt{\frac{\mu_{r2}}{\varepsilon_{r2}}} =120\pi\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}}=60\pi\Omega \]

电场透射系数：

\[ \tau=\frac{2\eta_2}{\eta_1+\eta_2} =\frac{2\cdot60\pi}{120\pi+60\pi} =0.667 \]

区域 2 中的相位常数：

\[ \beta_2=\frac{\omega}{c}\sqrt{\mu_{r2}\varepsilon_{r2}} =\frac{3\pi\times10^9}{3\times10^8}\cdot2 =20\pi\text{ rad/m} \]

透射电场幅值：

\[ E_{2m}=\tau E_{im}=0.667\times10=6.67\text{ V/m} \]

所以

\[ \boxed{\vec E_2=\vec e_x6.67\cos(3\pi\times10^9t-20\pi z)\text{ V/m}} \]

磁场幅值为 \(H_{2m}=E_{2m}/\eta_2\)：

\[ \boxed{\vec H_2=\vec e_y0.036\cos(3\pi\times10^9t-20\pi z)\text{ A/m}} \]

例题 8-13：两无耗介质正入射

原题

Given medium 1: \(\varepsilon_{r1}=4,\mu_{r1}=1,\sigma_1=0\); medium 2: \(\varepsilon_{r2}=10,\mu_{r2}=4,\sigma_2=0\). A uniform plane wave with angular frequency \(\omega=5\times10^8\text{ rad/s}\) vertically incidents to the interface from medium 1. The incidence wave is linearly polarized in \(x\) direction. When \(t=0,z=0\), the amplitude of incidence wave is \(2.4\text{ V/m}\). Find:

  1. \(\beta_1,\beta_2\);
  2. reflection coefficient \(\Gamma\) and transmission coefficient \(\tau\);
  3. electric field in region 1;
  4. electric field in region 2.

详细作答

相位常数：

\[ \beta_1=\frac{\omega}{c}\sqrt{\mu_{r1}\varepsilon_{r1}} =\frac{5\times10^8}{3\times10^8}\cdot2 =3.33\text{ rad/m} \]

\[ \beta_2=\frac{\omega}{c}\sqrt{\mu_{r2}\varepsilon_{r2}} =\frac{5\times10^8}{3\times10^8}\sqrt{40} =10.54\text{ rad/m} \]

波阻抗：

\[ \eta_1=\eta_0\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}}=60\pi\Omega \]

\[ \eta_2=\eta_0\sqrt{\frac{4}{10}}\approx75.9\pi\Omega \]

反射系数：

\[ \Gamma=\frac{\eta_2-\eta_1}{\eta_2+\eta_1} =\frac{75.9-60}{75.9+60}=0.117 \]

透射系数：

\[ \tau=\frac{2\eta_2}{\eta_1+\eta_2}\approx1.12 \]

入射电场幅值 \(E_{im}=2.4\text{ V/m}\)。区域 1 中有入射波和反射波：

\[ \vec E_1(z) =\vec e_xE_{im}\left(e^{-j\beta_1z}+\Gamma e^{j\beta_1z}\right) \]

代入数值：

\[ \vec E_1(z)=\vec e_x2.4e^{-j3.33z}+\vec e_x0.281e^{j3.33z} \]

瞬时形式：

\[ \boxed{ \vec E_1(z,t)= \vec e_x2.4\cos(5\times10^8t-3.33z) +\vec e_x0.281\cos(5\times10^8t+3.33z) } \]

区域 2 只有透射波：

\[ \vec E_2(z)=\vec e_x\tau E_{im}e^{-j\beta_2z} =\vec e_x2.68e^{-j10.54z} \]

瞬时形式：

\[ \boxed{\vec E_2(z,t)=\vec e_x2.68\cos(5\times10^8t-10.54z)\text{ V/m}} \]
